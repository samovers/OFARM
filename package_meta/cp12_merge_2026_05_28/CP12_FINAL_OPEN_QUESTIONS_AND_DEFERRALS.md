# CP12 final open questions and deferrals

## Open but non-blocking for CP12 acceptance

- Vendor-specific robot/machine payload profiles.
- External safety certification profile mapping.
- Field-pilot validation.
- Runtime implementation evidence.
- Full active pack-schema currentness integration.
- Advanced fleet coordination.
- Detailed liability and insurance mapping.

## Explicit deferrals


## Explicit deferrals

CP12 does not create:

- CP13 learning, experimentation, farm-memory, or learning-promotion law;
- CP14 farm-to-farm intelligence, regional mission coordination, or federated-learning law;
- CP15 generated-software delivery, robot-adapter deployment, rollback, or SBOM law;
- livestock-specific mission law;
- vendor protocol conformance;
- legal or safety certification.


## Boundary decision

CP12 is acceptable as mission-envelope law only. It must not be used as production robot readiness, legal safety certification, or vendor-protocol conformance.
