
# CP14 Final Migration Notes

Status: final CP14 migration-note candidate

Existing DataSovereigntyBoundary, SharingGrant, DelegationGrant, RevocationDecision, CrossPlatformGrantReceipt, ContractReference, AgentDataLearningPolicy, CP11 disclosure/claim controls, CP12 mission/incident controls, and CP13 learning/farm-memory controls remain valid under their original scope.

CP14 does not automatically convert any existing sharing grant into a FarmIntelligenceShareGrant. Existing cross-farm or partner-facing intelligence should be treated as Advisory and non-current-state unless explicitly reclassified under CP14.

Existing CP13 farm-memory, seasonal-learning, learning-output, or causal-estimate artifacts remain local/farm-scoped unless packaged through CP14 and evaluated through recipient-use, applicability, disclosure, and output-qualification gates.

Existing CP11 sustainability outputs and CP12 mission/incident records retain their original governance and disclosure limits. CP14 adds cross-farm sharing and recipient-use requirements; it does not weaken the underlying CP11/CP12 restrictions.

CP14 machine contracts remain draft/non-default until a separate currentness-promotion decision.
