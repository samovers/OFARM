Date: 2026-04-12

## Purpose

Wave 18 returns to a central post-amendment conformance gap rather than extending bridge-specific rehearsal.
The selected target is the identity/lifecycle cluster that remained partially covered or uncovered in the conformance matrix:
- identity versus revision across more families
- replacement and overlap lineage
- durable versus ephemeral zone treatment
- equipment / facility / storage / container lifecycle
- identity/lifecycle-triggered invalidation evidence

## Change class

This wave is strictly an `04_implementation_and_conformance/` hardening step.

It does **not**:
- amend baseline law
- amend accepted RFCs
- amend companion policy
- amend machine-contract substance

## Outcome

Wave 18 adds runtime-shaped lifecycle records, telemetry, and invalidation records sufficient to close the targeted central lifecycle rows while keeping broader invalidation proof honestly partial.
