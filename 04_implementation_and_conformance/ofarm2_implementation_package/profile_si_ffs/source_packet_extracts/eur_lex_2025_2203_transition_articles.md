# EUR-Lex 2025/2203 Operative Articles

Source: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg_impl/2025/2203/oj/eng

Extract scope: operative Articles 1 and 2, because Article 1 adds the transition paragraph to Implementing Regulation (EU) 2023/564 and Article 2 sets entry into force.

## Article 1

In Article 3 of Implementing Regulation (EU) 2023/564, the following paragraph is added:

‘For uses of plant protection products on their territory before 1 January 2027, Member States may allow that the respective records are not transferred into the prescribed electronic format.’

## Article 2

This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
