## 3. Farm recruitment one-pager (talking points)

- From 2026 the spray register must be electronic (EU rule); by **31 Jan 2027** last year's data must be entered into the state system — this app does both without double entry.
- You record in the field in under 90 seconds: product (picked from the official register), dose, parcel (your GERKs preloaded), done. Works offline.
- The app warns you (never blocks) if a product isn't authorized for the crop per the official register snapshot.
- For an inspection you hand over a generated register; gaps are listed honestly instead of hidden.
- Your data stays yours: nothing leaves the farm without your explicit grant; the advisor sees only what you share.
- Pilot = parallel running; keep your current method as backup all season.
