# OFARM Phase 8 — Scenario Governance Principles v0.1

## Principles

1. **Every scenario must preserve active OFARM law.** Scenario usefulness cannot justify bypassing evidence, authority, promotion, pack, sharing, query, output, or twin boundaries.
2. **Every scenario must include a hostile edge.** The fixture must state at least one action that must be blocked, downgraded, or qualified.
3. **Every scenario must state the farmer value.** A fixture must not exist only to exercise schemas.
4. **Every scenario must preserve output posture.** Draft, advisory, candidate, accepted, filed, and published outputs must remain distinct.
5. **Every scenario must preserve current-state qualification.** Briefs, summaries, and read-model outputs must carry freshness and permission limits.
6. **Every scenario must be traceable.** A future implementation must be able to trace who acted, under what authority, with what inputs, producing what outputs.
7. **Every scenario must be reviewable by a non-agent human.** Human review remains explicit where OFARM requires it.
