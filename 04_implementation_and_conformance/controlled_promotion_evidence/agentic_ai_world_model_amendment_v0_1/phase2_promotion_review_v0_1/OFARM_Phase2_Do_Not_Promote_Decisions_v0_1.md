# OFARM Phase 2 Do-Not-Promote Decisions v0.1

Status: supporting control record

Do not promote these in Phase 2:

## Calculation and formula material

Reason: quantity conversion, rate calculation, rounding, carrier volume, active ingredient, inventory drawdown, and formula catalog behavior can become hidden agronomic law if promoted before numeric fixtures and formula-governance review.

## Minimum capture profile material

Reason: minimum capture is useful for ordinary-farm usability, but it must not weaken evidence sufficiency, identity, authority, or high-consequence output gates.

## SDK, reference skeleton, OpenAPI/AsyncAPI runtime evidence, and two-agent execution material

Reason: these are implementation/conformance supports. They are not model/runtime law and cannot prove readiness before execution.

## AgentToolManifest

Reason: promising, but it should be harmonized in Phase 5 with AgentRunEnvelope, AgentRunTrace, AgentHandoffEnvelope, Capability Manifest agentSupport, and autonomy/action-class policy.

## Conformance execution reports

Reason: no implementation exists. Reports may define tests, but must not claim executed conformance.
