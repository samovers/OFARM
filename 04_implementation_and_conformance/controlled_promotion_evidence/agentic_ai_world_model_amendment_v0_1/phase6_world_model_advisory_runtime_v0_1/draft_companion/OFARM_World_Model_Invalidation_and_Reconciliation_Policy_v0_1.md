# OFARM World Model Invalidation and Reconciliation Policy v0.1

## Status

Draft companion candidate. Supporting only.

## Invalidation

World-model state and scenario results must declare what makes them stale, invalid, or review-required.

Common triggers include time expiry, new observations, stale observations, forecast divergence, crop-stage change, management actions outside scenario assumptions, pack/profile changes, model-version supersession, materialization staleness, evidence conflict, and sharing or authority changes.

## Reconciliation

A reconciliation record compares scenario output to later observations. It supports calibration and model-quality governance. It must not correct canonical history, accept compliance facts, or resolve disputes by itself.
