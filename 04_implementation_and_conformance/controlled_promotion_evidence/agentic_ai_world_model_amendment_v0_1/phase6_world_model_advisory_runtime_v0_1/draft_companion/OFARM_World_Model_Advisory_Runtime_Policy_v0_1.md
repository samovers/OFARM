# OFARM World Model Advisory Runtime Policy v0.1

## Status

Draft companion candidate. Supporting only.

## Policy

World-model runtime capabilities must operate inside the Advisory Twin and must preserve the same semantic substrate as the rest of OFARM.

World-model outputs may help farmers understand risk, rehearse decisions, prioritize scouting, identify evidence gaps, prepare review packets, and propose BridgeCandidates. They may not become hidden truth, hidden current state, or hidden governance.

## Required result display posture

Any farmer-facing display of world-model material must preserve:

- Advisory-only status;
- uncertainty;
- evidence gaps;
- validity window;
- invalidation status;
- output disposition;
- human-review requirements;
- blocked high-consequence uses.
