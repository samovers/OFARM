# OFARM Phase 7 — Request Satisfaction and Review Rules v0.1

## Satisfaction rule

A request is satisfied only when its completion criteria are met by acceptable artifacts, observations, local annotations, or governed review.

## Review rule

Satisfaction may still require human review if:

- the request is compliance-blocking;
- evidence sufficiency is at stake;
- authority/delegation is unclear;
- the captured artifact is weak, conflicting, stale, or external;
- local narrative conflicts with sensor or contractor records;
- the request affects a frozen output, filing, attestation, or accepted promotion path.

## Waiver rule

A request may be waived only through a governed review decision. A waiver does not create evidence and must preserve the unresolved gap unless the review decision explicitly closes it.
