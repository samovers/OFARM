# OFARM Phase 7 — EvidenceNeed Rules v0.1

## Definition

`EvidenceNeed` is a candidate OFARM object that states that an OFARM workflow, claim, scenario, output, promotion path, pack rule, or review path lacks sufficient evidence.

## Required meaning

An `EvidenceNeed` must identify:

- the blocked or affected decision/claim/output;
- the evidence insufficiency;
- the reason the gap matters;
- acceptable evidence options;
- whether external evidence is mandatory before promotion;
- severity and priority;
- consequence if unresolved;
- relevance window;
- completion criteria;
- request source;
- current lifecycle state.

## Non-goals

`EvidenceNeed` is not:

- evidence;
- accepted fact;
- attestation;
- compliance certification;
- proof that a real-world condition exists;
- a waiver of OFARM evidence sufficiency policy.
