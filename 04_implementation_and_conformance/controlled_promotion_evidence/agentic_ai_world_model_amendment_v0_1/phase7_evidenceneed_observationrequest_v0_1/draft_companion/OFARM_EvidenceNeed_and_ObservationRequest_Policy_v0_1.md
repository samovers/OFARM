# OFARM EvidenceNeed and ObservationRequest Policy v0.1

Status: draft companion candidate; not active until promoted.

## Policy

`EvidenceNeed` and `ObservationRequest` are request-layer artifacts. They make missing information actionable, traceable, and bounded. They do not create accepted truth, accepted observations, compliance facts, or governance decisions.

## Request-source classes

Requests may originate from human review, agent runs, world-model runs, preflight checks, pack/evidence policies, output assembly previews, imports, or current-state freshness checks.

## Required display posture

A farmer-facing surface must preserve request posture, priority, burden, deadline/relevance, consequence, and whether the request is advisory, operational, evidence-related, or compliance-blocking.
