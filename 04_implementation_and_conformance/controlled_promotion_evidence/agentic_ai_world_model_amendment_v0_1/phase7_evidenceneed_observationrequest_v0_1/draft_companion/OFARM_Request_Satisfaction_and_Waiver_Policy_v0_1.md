# OFARM Request Satisfaction and Waiver Policy v0.1

Status: draft companion candidate; not active until promoted.

## Satisfaction

A request is satisfied when its completion criteria are met and any required review has occurred. Satisfaction may create candidate evidence, accepted evidence, review records, or no promotable artifact depending on normal OFARM governance.

## Waiver

Waiver is a review outcome. It must preserve the basis for why the request was waived and must not imply that missing evidence existed.

## Expiry and supersession

Expired or superseded requests remain traceable and may still be relevant to dispute reconstruction or output qualification.
