# OFARM Farmer Burden and Request Noise Control Policy v0.1

Status: draft companion candidate; not active until promoted.

## Policy

Generated requests must be proportionate. The platform should not overwhelm farmers with repetitive, low-value, vague, stale, or unprioritized AI tasks.

## Controls

- require priority;
- require burden estimate;
- require relevance window;
- require consequence;
- require deduplication key;
- collapse duplicate requests;
- suppress requests that are stale, already satisfied, or outside purpose;
- escalate only when an explicit basis supports escalation.
