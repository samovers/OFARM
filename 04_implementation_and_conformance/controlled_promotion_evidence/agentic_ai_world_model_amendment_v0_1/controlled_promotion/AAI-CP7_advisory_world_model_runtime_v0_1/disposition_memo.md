# CP7 disposition memo

Decision: promote after targeted revision, as a bounded contract layer only.

CP7 adopts the Deep Research recommendation to promote world-model contracts cautiously while keeping stronger readiness claims prohibited. The phase promotes the contract shapes needed for Advisory Twin scenario execution and conformance testing, but it does not assert that deployed world-model monitoring, calibration, uncertainty thresholds, or farmer-facing comprehension are complete.

The promoted concepts are deliberately narrow:

- governed simulation run;
- advisory state;
- input basis;
- observation basis;
- assumption set;
- uncertainty statement;
- validity window;
- invalidation rule;
- output disposition;
- governance blocker;
- reconciliation record;
- scenario specification;
- scenario result set.

Held concepts:

- `WorldModelCalibrationEvidence` as a dedicated contract;
- EvidenceNeed and ObservationRequest;
- farmer-facing request-burden policy;
- world-model readiness claim;
- production/live monitoring sufficiency.
