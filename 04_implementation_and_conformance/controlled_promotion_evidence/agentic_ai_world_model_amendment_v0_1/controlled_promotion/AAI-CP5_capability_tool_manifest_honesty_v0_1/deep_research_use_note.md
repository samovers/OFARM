# CP5 Deep Research use note

The attached Deep Research report was used as supporting research, not as active law. CP5 adopted its recommendation to promote manifest-honesty after targeted revision, while keeping advisory world-model runtime, EvidenceNeed, ObservationRequest, and farmer-facing AI workflow conformance in later/experiment-first phases.

Deep Research themes incorporated:

- manifests are descriptive, not dispositive;
- tool annotations and declared hints are untrusted until verified;
- manifest ecosystems can be attacked and overclaimed;
- tool success is not governance success;
- every readiness claim should reference evidence or expire;
- hidden side effects, hidden egress, false read-only claims, and missing trace fields require hostile fixtures.
