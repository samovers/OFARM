# CP5 disposition memo

CP5 is promoted as a bounded active subset because CP2, CP3, and CP4 now provide the necessary public-surface, result-qualification, actorship, authority, run, trace, blocked-action, and handoff context.

The attached Deep Research report supported promotion after targeted revision for manifest-honesty, while warning against treating manifests as authority, trust, or readiness proof. CP5 incorporates that by requiring evidence-qualified readiness claims, untrusted hint handling, side-effect classification, external-call policy, redaction policy, trace retention, and data-learning policy.

The phase does not promote world-model support, EvidenceNeed, ObservationRequest, output preview, runtime readiness, or two-agent compatibility.
