# CP8 disposition memo

## Disposition

`BOUNDED_ACTIVE_REQUEST_LAYER_PROMOTION_WITH_EXPERIMENT_FIRST_UX_LIMITS`.

## Decision

Promote the EvidenceNeed/ObservationRequest RFC, the farmer-burden/noise/display companion policy, and active request-layer machine contracts. Do not promote minimum capture profile law, formula defaults, output preview, or any readiness claim.

## Rationale

The active baseline already forbids AI outputs, memory, requests, and world-model state from becoming hidden truth or hidden governance. CP8 makes that safer by requiring every request-layer artifact to expose non-evidence, non-obligation, and non-blocker-by-itself semantics plus burden, relevance, display, lifecycle, deduplication, and satisfaction traces.

## Residual risk

Farmer comprehension, request fatigue, stale-data comprehension, live revocation handling, and dispute reconstruction remain pilot/experiment evidence topics. CP8 provides contracts and static conformance, not production UX proof.
