## 1. Purpose

This fixture set proves the smallest bridge-handoff closure now promoted into active substance:
- BridgeCandidate is machine-visible and proposal-shaped
- the source twin remains Advisory
- human approval remains mandatory
- Compliance-targeted next steps explicitly declare freshness recheck posture
- the promoted seam stays narrow and does not silently promote the rest of the scenario-workspace object family

## 3. Executable fixture families

### 3.1 Compliance-targeted bridge handoff
The runner must prove that a Compliance-targeted BridgeCandidate:
- originates in Advisory
- stays proposal-shaped
- requires human approval
- declares Compliance freshness recheck explicitly
- may point to a draft planned intervention without becoming executed truth

### 3.2 Advisory-targeted evidence request
The runner must prove that an Advisory-targeted BridgeCandidate can still request bounded additional evidence without mutating Compliance state.

### 3.3 Negative guardrail behavior
The runner must prove that:
- removing the human-approval requirement fails validation
- sending a Compliance-targeted bridge forward without Compliance freshness recheck fails validation
