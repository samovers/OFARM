# OFARM AI-Agent-Ready Platform Phase 4 Research Intake v0.1

Date: 2026-05-13  
Status: active supporting research intake summary  
Source: `deep-research-report-25.md` uploaded in conversation on 2026-05-13

## Purpose

This intake records how the attached research was used for Phase 4 of the AI-agent-ready platform amendment.

The research is not active OFARM law. It informs implementation/conformance artifacts only. Baseline law and accepted RFCs still govern.

## Adopted for Phase 4

Phase 4 adopts these research-backed patterns as draft implementation support:

- public read surfaces need a mandatory result-qualification envelope
- stale, disputed, redacted, candidate-only, permission-limited, and evidence-insufficient states must remain first-class app states
- output previews must be explicit and non-authoritative
- compiled outputs must not become truth stores
- public schema surfaces should be closed and machine-readable
- conformance should test display honesty, not only protocol shape

## Not promoted by this phase

Phase 4 does not promote:

- external standards as OFARM law
- a CRUD façade
- direct mutation of current-state materialization stores
- FMIS/machinery/sensor imports as accepted truth
- calculation formulas or unit conversion policy
- SDK or reference platform skeleton

## Follow-on phases

The research recommendations on unit/formula governance, candidate-first import, SDK/codegen, conformance test plans, and offline sync should be handled in later phases.
