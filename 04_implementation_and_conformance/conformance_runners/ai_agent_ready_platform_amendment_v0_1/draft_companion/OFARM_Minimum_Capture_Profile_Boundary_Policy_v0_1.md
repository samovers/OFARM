# OFARM Minimum Capture Profile Boundary Policy v0.1

Date: 2026-05-13  
Status: draft companion candidate; non-default implementation support

## Purpose

This policy defines how an ordinary-farm minimum capture profile may be piloted without weakening OFARM evidence, authority, identity, or high-consequence output gates.

## Boundary

Minimum capture is not a shortcut to truth. It is a way to preserve usable drafts, candidate records, and low-consequence advisory context when perfect data entry is unavailable.

## Non-default status

Minimum capture profiles remain `DRAFT_NON_DEFAULT` or `PILOT_ONLY` until owner/live evidence and conformance review support promotion.

## Allowed uses

- low-consequence capture
- advisory preview
- import candidate preparation
- compliance precheck only
- pilot-only workflows

## Blocked uses when required fields are missing

- accepted compliance truth
- frozen DocumentAssembly
- inventory mutation
- high-consequence recommendation/prescription execution
- current-state materialization used as compliance basis

## Required app behavior

Apps must show missing, late-bound, evidence-insufficient, and review-required states. They must not silently default unknown product, actor, area, or time values.
