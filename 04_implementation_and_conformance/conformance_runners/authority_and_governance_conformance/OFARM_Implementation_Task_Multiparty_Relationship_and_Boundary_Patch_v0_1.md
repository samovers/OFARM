## 1. Why this note exists

The targeted design pass narrowed the remaining debt to two concrete seams:
- OFARM needed an executable durable relationship layer for recurring multiparty realities such as owner–tenant–operator, cooperative membership/admin, advisor-of-record, seasonal labour, pooled responsibility, and emergency helper designation.
- OFARM needed explicit starter sister-platform boundary artifacts for contract-linked access and cross-platform grant traceability without silently becoming marketplace, payments, or social truth.

This note records the executed narrow patch waves and the one remaining queued follow-up lane.

## 5. Risks and stop conditions

### 5.1 Main risks

- relationship artifacts drift into shadow permissions
- boundary artifacts drift into order-management or payment truth
- advisory signals drift into a second evidence or social lane
- conformance starts relying on implied power instead of explicit grants

### 5.2 Stop conditions

Stop and re-scope if any follow-on work requires:
- replacing `RoleAssignment`, `AuthorityGrant`, `DelegationGrant`, or `SharingGrant`
- changing assertion/history-first or current-state law
- introducing native order, invoice, settlement, or payment truth into OFARM
- reopening the Advisory/Compliance twin split
