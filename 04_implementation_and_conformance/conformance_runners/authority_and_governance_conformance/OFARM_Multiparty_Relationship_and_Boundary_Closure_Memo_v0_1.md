## 5. Reopen triggers

Reopen this line only if one or more of the following occurs:

1. at least two real advisory or monitoring integrations converge on the same minimum signal carrier
2. delivery/dispute traffic can no longer be expressed cleanly with the current profile-first approach
3. a pilot or integration exposes permission leakage from relationship artifacts
4. cross-platform grant receipts prove too weak for revocation, expiry, or audit handling
5. repeated implementation pressure proves a missing reusable carrier that cannot be handled by the active family set

Absent one of these triggers, further package-internal expansion would be over-modeling.
