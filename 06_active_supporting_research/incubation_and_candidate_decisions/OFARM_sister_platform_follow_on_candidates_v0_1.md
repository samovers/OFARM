# OFARM sister-platform follow-on candidates v0.1

Date: 2026-04-21  
Status: active supporting research artifact  
Scope: keep visible the next plausible sister-platform follow-on candidates after the durable relationship patch, starter exchange-boundary patch, and the first high-level linked-domain framing wave

---

## 1. Why this note exists

The current package now has:
- durable relationship semantics in the accepted RFC lane
- starter exchange-boundary contracts in the accepted RFC lane
- hostile multiparty fixtures in implementation/conformance
- OFARM-side sister-platform boundary policy
- linked-domain family framing and constitutional tenets for OFARM Exchange and OFARM Social

That is enough to make the architectural picture explicit.
It is not the end of all boundary work.

This note keeps the remaining candidate lanes visible without over-promoting them.

---

## 2. Candidate lanes

### 2.1 Social signal boundary starter contracts
Current confidence: medium  
Likely target: `04_implementation_and_conformance/` first, with later promotion only if a real shared minimal contract stabilizes

Why not promoted now:
- the current package still has enough via advisory bridge law, review posture, and the queued advisory-signal boundary candidate lane
- no real integration yet proves a stable minimal cross-platform signal object family

Trigger to reopen:
- at least two real social/advisory/monitoring integrations converge on the same minimal required fields
- or live implementation proves the current bridge/review law insufficient

### 2.2 Cross-platform identity, consent, and recipient-purpose tightening
Current confidence: medium-high  
Likely target: companion clarification first, RFC only if cross-platform identity binding becomes materially ambiguous

Why not promoted now:
- `ContractReference`, `CrossPlatformGrantReceipt`, and existing OFARM publication/sharing law already close the smallest current seam
- deeper identity-consent law is best triggered by a real deployment or a concrete contradiction

Trigger to reopen:
- first real Exchange or Social implementation that needs multi-platform identity binding beyond current grant/reference posture

### 2.3 Publication-basis visibility tightening for recipient-facing outputs
Current confidence: high  
Likely target: companion clarification or hostile fixture wave

Why not promoted now:
- the current package already has strong currentness/materialization law
- the remaining question is mainly how visibly the relied-on basis must travel on more recipient-facing outputs by default

Trigger to reopen:
- real buyer or regulator pilot where stale-view misunderstanding becomes likely

### 2.4 Cooperative pooled-service refinement
Current confidence: medium  
Likely target: implementation/conformance clarification first

Why not promoted now:
- the durable relationship RFC already covers membership/admin/pooling at the bounded current level
- no current contradiction forces a separate cooperative-specific RFC wave

Trigger to reopen:
- real cooperative implementation shows the current relationship kinds are too coarse for service pooling, pooled branding, or dispute handling

### 2.5 Deeper OFARM Exchange constitution
Current confidence: high that it will eventually be useful  
Likely target: `07_linked_domain_architectures/` first, then a dedicated exchange repository when implementation begins

Why not promoted now:
- the package only needs tenets and OFARM-side boundary law at this stage
- a full exchange constitution without real product truth, contract workflow, or implementation triggers would be speculative

Trigger to reopen:
- first real Exchange prototype, pilot, or implementation charter

### 2.6 Deeper OFARM Social constitution
Current confidence: medium  
Likely target: `07_linked_domain_architectures/` first, then a dedicated social/community repository when implementation begins

Why not promoted now:
- current value comes from making the boundary explicit, not from prematurely freezing moderation or community-law details
- the queued advisory-signal lane already holds the most immediate OFARM-facing integration seam

Trigger to reopen:
- first real community/social implementation trigger, especially one involving moderation, group governance, or signal ingestion

---

## 3. Guardrail

None of the candidate lanes in this note should silently re-enter OFARM active law without an explicit patch wave.
The purpose of this note is visibility, not covert promotion.
